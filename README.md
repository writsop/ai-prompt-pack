## 💡 Example

**Template:**
```
Act as a senior [ROLE] with 15 years of experience. I need you to [TASK]. 
My context: [CONTEXT]. Please provide [OUTPUT FORMAT].
```

**Filled in:**
```
Act as a senior marketing strategist with 15 years of experience. I need you to 
create a go-to-market plan for a B2B SaaS product. My context: We're launching 
a project management tool for remote teams, $29/mo pricing, targeting 50-200 
employee companies. Please provide a 90-day launch plan with channels, budget 
allocation, and KPIs.
```
